## Revenue Analysis
I included 7 things in Revenue Analysis Dashboard :
- Total Revenue
- Revenue by Market
- Total Sales
- Sales by Market
- Revenue per Year
- Top 5 Products
- Top 5 Customers

These parameters and their graphs can be specified by time:
- Year
- Month
