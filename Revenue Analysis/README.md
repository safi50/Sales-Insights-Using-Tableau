## Profit Analysis
I included 7 things in Profit Analysis Dashboard :
- Total Revenue
- Revenue by Market
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin by Market
- Profit Trend
- Customer and their Sales and Profit
- Customer Types

These parameters and their graphs can be specified by time:
- Year
- Month
